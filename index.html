Built for two people, never two identical bodies

A private conversation about how you can train.

A guided interview will hand you between medical safety, physiotherapy, exercise physiology, nutrition, strength, swimming, cycling, recovery and product-design perspectives. It asks one question at a time and builds a structured brief for your fully custom app.

This is not emergency care, diagnosis, treatment or exercise clearance. In Australia, call 000 for an emergency. The interview will pause when answers suggest urgent assessment or professional review.

Private by design

No account, analytics, advertising, server form or cloud health database. Answers remain in this browser until you export them.

Adaptive interview

The path changes around injuries, weight-management preferences, swimming, cycling, equipment and safety responses.

Structured handoff

Review every answer, then create an AES-GCM encrypted response packet to upload into the private project chat.

Who is completing the interview?

Each participant should complete and export their own interview. Do not fill in another person’s medical history for them.
